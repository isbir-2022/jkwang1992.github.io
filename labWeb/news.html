  • Lee Awards

    Postgraduate student Lo Po Wen Frank received The Lee Family Scholarship 2017

    Mr. Lo Po Wen Frank, postgraduate student from Department of Electronic Engineering, supervised by Professor Max Q.-H. Meng, received The Lee Family Scholarship 2017 on 22nd Mar, 2017. The scholarship is well known for its competitiveness, there are only 2 awardees this year in Hong Kong. The scholarship will provide full funding for tuition fees, a stipend of £20,600 per annum to assist with living costs for 3.5 years and £2,000 towards travel and/or conference support for the first three years. Mr. Lo Po Wen Frank will start his PhD study at Faculty of Medicine of Imperial College London from this October under the sponsorship of the scholarship. Congratulations!
